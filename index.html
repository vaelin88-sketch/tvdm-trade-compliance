Services

What I can do for your business

Flexible trade compliance services tailored to your needs — from one-off projects to ongoing management.
01

Customs & Trade Compliance Management

Classification, valuation, origin determination, customs declarations, and ongoing compliance monitoring. I manage your customs operations so you can focus on your business.

02

Export Control & Sanctions

Export license applications (DCSG), dual-use classification, sanctioned party screening, license management in SAP GTS, and regulatory reporting. Stay compliant, keep shipping.

03

AEO & Authorization Management

Full project management for AEO-C/AEO-S certification, approved exporter status, end-use authorizations, and bonded warehouse permits. From application to approval.

04

Regulatory Compliance (Chemical/Pharma)

Drug precursors, hormonal substances, ODS, PIC, chemical weapons conventions. Specialized in the regulatory maze of the chemical and pharmaceutical sectors.

05

SAP GTS Implementation & Support

Configuration and optimization of SAP GTS for customs, export control, sanctioned party screening, and sales order compliance. Get the most out of your investment.

06

Trade Compliance Audits & Remediation

Comprehensive audits to identify risk areas and cost savings, followed by hands-on fixes. Also includes Power BI dashboard development for ongoing trade data insights.

07

Excise & Energy Tax Advisory

Advisory and project management for excise duties, energy taxes, and licensing. Specialized experience in bio-fuel and chemical sector requirements.

08

Interim Compliance Management

Need temporary coverage? I step in as your in-house compliance manager during transitions, parental leave, or while you search for a permanent hire. Full operational handover included.
